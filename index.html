Pizza Heaven

  • It's Responsive. Try expanding and shrinking the window.
  • The wings flap. Click on pizza halo to restart the animation.

  • Custom popup when No Cheese selected and Extra Cheese pressed.
  • Custom smooth scroll to total price when View Price pressed.
  • Not required to click radio button or checkbox. Can click menu item.
  • Clicking on a menu item adds it to the receipt and recalculates total price.
